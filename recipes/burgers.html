Burgers

Description

This recipe will help you make delicious burgers that will taste amazing - no matter what Sidney may say!

Please read the whole recipe before starting! You won't want to have to repeat any steps because you weren't ready for them.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Hamburger buns
  • Optional toppings: lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, ketchup, mustard

Steps

  1. Preheat grill or stovetop grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  3. Divide the mixture into equal portions and shape into burger patties.
  4. Place burger patties on the grill and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until desired doneness is reached.
  5. Toast hamburger buns on the grill for a minute or until lightly golden.
  6. Assemble burgers by placing the cooked patties on the bottom buns and adding your favorite toppings.
  7. Top with the remaining bun halves and serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy your delicious homemade burgers!
