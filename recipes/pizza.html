Pizza

Description

This recipe will help you make delicious pizza that certainly will not come out of the oven underbaked - regardless of what Sidney may say!

However, make sure the follow the recipe exactly or you may have to start again from scratch! And don't worry if you aren't a pro-cook, this one is easy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Assorted toppings: sliced pepperoni, sliced mushrooms, sliced bell peppers, sliced onions, sliced olives, etc.
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Optional seasonings: dried oregano, dried basil, garlic powder

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 450°F (230°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a baking sheet or pizza stone.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Add your desired toppings evenly over the cheese.
  7. Drizzle the olive oil over the toppings and crust.
  8. If desired, sprinkle with dried oregano, dried basil, and garlic powder for extra flavor.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
